Past Fair Highlights

Winter Innovation Fair 2024

December 15, 2024 Renton Community Center 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Our most successful fair to date featured 85 young entrepreneurs showcasing innovative tech solutions, sustainable products, and creative services. The theme focused on "Innovation for Tomorrow," inspiring participants to think about future challenges and solutions.

Highlights included a 12-year-old's solar-powered phone charger, a teen's upcycled fashion line, and a group's community garden planning service. The fair raised over $8,000 in youth sales and attracted 1,200+ visitors.

Fall Harvest Market 2024

October 20, 2024 Liberty Park 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Our outdoor autumn fair celebrated seasonal entrepreneurship with 70 participants focusing on harvest-themed businesses. From homemade preserves to hand-crafted decorations, young entrepreneurs embraced the fall spirit while learning valuable business skills.

Special features included a pumpkin carving competition, apple cider tasting, and mentorship booths where successful local business owners shared insights with aspiring young entrepreneurs.

Summer Innovation Camp 2024

July 12-14, 2024 Renton Technical College 3-Day Program

Our first multi-day program combined intensive business workshops with a culminating fair experience. 60 participants spent three days learning business fundamentals, developing products, creating marketing materials, and preparing presentations.

The program featured guest speakers from local startups, hands-on workshops in digital marketing, financial literacy sessions, and partnership with Renton Technical College's entrepreneurship program. The final day showcase attracted over 800 community members.

Spring Startup Showcase 2024

April 6, 2024 Downtown Renton Plaza 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Our spring event focused on tech-enabled businesses and social impact ventures. 55 young entrepreneurs presented everything from mobile apps to community service platforms. This fair introduced our first-ever "Social Impact Award" for businesses addressing community challenges.

Notable highlights included a 14-year-old's app for connecting elderly community members with young volunteers, a group's recycling awareness campaign with incentive rewards, and several food businesses featuring international cuisines reflecting our diverse community.
