Past Fair Highlights

Cascade Family Fun Day

3/7/2026 Impact | Black River Elementary 12:00pm - 3:00pm

Our latest Children's Business Fair took place during Cascade Family Fun Day, bringing young entrepreneurs right into the heart of the school community of Impact | Black River Elementary.

Young vendors displayed their creativity and business skills while families enjoyed a day of fun and learning. The integration with the Family Fun Day allowed for maximum community engagement and support for our budding entrepreneurs.

Mid Winter Fair

3/29/2025 Renton Technical College 12:00pm - 3:00pm

Our first Children's Business Fair was a huge success, featuring 26 young vendors showcasing their creativity and hard work. The indoor venue buzzed with over 150 visitors supporting these budding entrepreneurs.

From 3D-printed fidgets and board games to homemade desserts, crepes, and handmade décor, every booth had something unique to offer. The kids sold, smiled, and shined, turning their big ideas into real-world experiences.

Summer Fair

7/26/25 Honey Dew Elementary 12:00pm - 3:00pm

Our July Children's Business Fair was an incredible outdoor event with 50 young vendors and over 200 visitors. Representatives from the city and local businesses joined in to support and celebrate these young entrepreneurs.

From savory foods and honey to elf ears, one-of-a-kind candles, and youth-authored books, creativity was everywhere. It was inspiring to see returning vendors grow in confidence and first-time sellers bravely step into the world of business.

Pre-Holiday Market

10/25/25 Junior Achievement of Washington 12:00pm - 3:00pm

Our last Children’s Business Fair of 2025 was our biggest yet: 80 young vendors indoor and over 300 visitors filling the space with energy and excitement. We even had a special visit from the Seahawks’ mascot, making the day extra memorable! Partnering with Junior Achievement of Washington allowed us to introduce more ways for youth to grow their entrepreneurial skills. With face painting, balloon art, board games, food, crafts, and books, the fair truly showcased creativity, community, and the spirit of young entrepreneurship.
