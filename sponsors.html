Why Sponsor Us?

Becoming a sponsor of the Renton Children's Business Fair is more than supporting a single event — it's investing in the next generation of thinkers, doers, and community leaders. Your partnership demonstrates your commitment to community wellness and showcases your organization as a champion of local youth development.

Empower Young Entrepreneurs

Support youth ages 6–17 with real-world business experience

Strengthen Education

Offer hands-on financial literacy to local families and schools

Build Confidence

Foster resilience and creativity in kids across the Pacific Northwest

Create Safe Spaces

Support community-centered environments where youth learn through action

Promote Your Brand

Connect with hundreds of parents, educators, and community members

Demonstrate Community Leadership

Showcase your commitment to community wellbeing and position your organization as a local champion invested in our youth's future

Sponsorship Levels

Community Sponsor

$500 Per Event

Perfect for small businesses wanting visibility & community impact

  • Booth at one fair
  • Logo on event signage
  • One social media thank-you post
  • Sponsor recognition during awards
  • Logo on website "Thank You Sponsors" page

Annual Supporter

$1,500 Per Year

For businesses wanting consistent visibility across multiple events

  • Booth at all fairs (3+ fairs a year)
  • Logo on t-shirts (if available that cycle)
  • 1 dedicated sponsor feature post on social media
  • Logo on printed event materials
  • Inclusion in vendor welcome emails
  • Invitation to judge or speak (optional)

Youth Enabler

$3,500 Per Year

Ideal for organizations focused on education, youth development, or community outreach

  • Logo on every fair banner, flyer, and digital poster
  • Booth at all fairs
  • Opportunity to host a kids activity or workshop
  • Year-round website presence
  • Featured sponsor spotlight story (video or written)
  • Ability to sponsor an award category
  • Monthly social media mentions (Mar–Oct)
